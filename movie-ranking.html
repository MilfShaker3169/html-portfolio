The Best Movies according to Rotten Tomatoes

Ranking of Top 3 Films

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is a 2001 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. The film follows the story of Chihiro Ogino, a sullen ten-year-old girl who, while moving to a new neighborhood, enters the world of Kami (spirits) and must find her way to save her parents and return to the human world. The film is widely regarded as one of the greatest animated films ever made, and it won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003.

Parasite

Parasite is a 2019 South Korean black comedy thriller film directed by Bong Joon-ho. The film follows the story of the Kim family, who lives in poverty, and the Park family, who lives in wealth. The film explores the themes of class struggle and social inequality.

blade runner 2049

Blade Runner 2049 is a 2017 American neo-noir science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve. The film is a sequel to the 1982 film Blade Runner and follows the story of a new blade runner, LAPD Officer K, who uncovers a long-buried secret that has the potential to plunge what’s left of society into chaos. The film explores themes of identity, memory, and the nature of humanity.
